About the Campaign

Green Earth is a global tree plantation initiative dedicated to fighting climate change. Since our start, we’ve planted over 500,000 trees
worldwide. By joining our campaign, you help restore forests, create
habitats for wildlife, and combat global warming.

  • Restoration of natural habitats
  • Improvement of air quality
  • Support for local communities
